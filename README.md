      <section>
        <h2>About Me</h2>
        <p>
          My name is Muhammad Danish Yaqoob. I am a researcher affiliated with
          Southern Medical University. My research interests include
          photodynamic therapy, dermatology, venerology, infectious skin
          diseases, narrative medicine, and translational biomedical research.
        </p>
        <p>
          My current work includes studies on photodynamic therapy mechanisms,
          cutaneous fungal infection models, chronic urticaria, plantar wart
          treatment, and interdisciplinary public health topics.
        </p>
      </section>
